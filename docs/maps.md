## AllTrails

[AllTrails](https://www.alltrails.com/) is a popular hiking app in the US and has some coverage of Japanese trails.
* If you cannot read any Japanese, this is your best bet.
* Great App & hiking community
* AllTrails lacks details (restrooms, lunch spots, etc.) for Japanese mountains.


## YAMAP

[YAMAP](https://yamap.com/) is one of the most popular Japanese hiking apps and the only one that has any English support.
* Core features are free and have English support.
* Premium features (like multiple offline maps) require a paid subscription.
* Apps and community content are nearly all in Japanese ğŸ¥²
* Amazing content as many hikers include trail conditions, transport information, and pictures in their hiking posts.
* YAMAP maps contain vital info like restrooms, benches, and bus stops.
